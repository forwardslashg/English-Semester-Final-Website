Evidence #3: Writing and Skill Development

Areas of Growth

Throughout this semester, the area where I experienced the most significant academic growth was in structural text evaluation and thesis defense. At the start of Grade 9, my paragraphs focused heavily on basic plot summaries—essentially just repeating what happened in the story rather than evaluating the author's message.

By focusing on peer feedback loops and analyzing paragraph structures during our multi-week short story units, I learned how to weave direct text quotes seamlessly into my arguments. This development helped me transition from simple opinion pieces to clear, evidence-backed arguments.
