About The Course

Grade 9 English focuses on building essential high school skills in reading, writing, and critical thinking.

Throughout the year, our class emphasized analyzing various literary genres, including novels, short stories, and drama. These readings served as the foundation for practicing structured paragraph writing and standard multi-paragraph essay layouts.

My Experience

Moving into high school English felt like a big transition. This course challenged me to look beyond basic plot summaries and think critically about why an author sets up a story a certain way.

Studying main course texts like Drew Hayden Taylor's *Motorcycles & Sweetgrass* helped me learn to back up my writing with solid examples directly from the text instead of guessing. I am proud of how much my confidence has grown when analyzing literature.
