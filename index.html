About The Course

Grade 9 English is strategically engineered to build foundational secondary-school competencies across critical text evaluation, precise evidence assembly, and rhetoric.

Throughout the academic term, our syllabus prioritized multi-genre literacy, requiring deep encounters with canonical and contemporary novels, short stories, and complex drama. These readings served as the base for constructing advanced paragraph templates and formal multi-paragraph compositions.

My Intellectual Journey

Transitioning into high school English brought forward an immediate shift in academic expectations. The primary challenge was moving entirely beyond basic plot recall toward unearthing an author's subtextual intent.

Engaging deeply with central course texts, such as Drew Hayden Taylor's *Motorcycles & Sweetgrass*, forced me to challenge surface assumptions and approach issues like cultural identity and generational storytelling with analytical nuance. My writing evolved from opinionated summaries into sound, evidence-backed arguments.
