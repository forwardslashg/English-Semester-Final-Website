About The Course

Grade 9 English focuses on building foundational high school skills in reading, writing, and critical thinking. Throughout the year, the curriculum emphasizes analyzing various literary genres, including novels, short stories, and drama, while developing structured paragraph and essay-writing techniques.

My Experience
